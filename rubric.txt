CEN 4072: Software Testing Spring 2026
Final Project report format
As the deliverable for the course, students will apply their knowledge of software testing in teams of 2 or 3:
1.	Create a test suite for a software system
a.	Your project could be an application of Integration, System, End-to-end, Acceptance or regression testing
b.	Must include at least 8-unit tests (in separate classes)
c.	Each Unit Tests should have at least 5 well defined methods
2.	Must include a combined test code(integration) using any testing tool for all unit tests- (TestNG.xml)
3.	The system under test may be a real-world website or web application. Students should identify and address possible automation constraints such as alerts, captchas/bot protection, pop-ups, or dynamic elements while designing their automation tests.
______________________________________________________________________________
Grading of the project will consist of 2 parts: an in-class demonstration and a report submission per team.
Part 1: In- Class Demonstration (60%) 
Power Point Presentation Slides:

Slide 5:

Last Slide: Conclusion and future works, TestNG reports and add references after this last slide.
After the presentation you need to show all your classes, execution and all passed test cases.
Note: You must screen record your work, and you will have to use it if your execution gets delayed (must) and you may reorder the slides as per your requirements and that is fine.
